Academic Programs and Courses
================================

This micro-site is aimed at helping prospective, current, and past students find information 
about the academic programs and courses at  `Computer Science Department <https://luc.edu/cs/>`_
at `Loyola University Chicago <https://luc.edu>`_.

.. note:: The authoritative source of current course information is `LOCUS <https://locus.luc.edu/>`_. This especially applies to degree requirements, course descriptions, and schedule information.
